  <name>Limitations</name>
  <action>Write ~150 words with honest limitations. Four specific limitations:

(1) Unit coverage: 13 SI units across 5 domains -- far fewer than CQE's 531-unit dictionary. The conversion table covers the most common scientific units but does not handle derived units (e.g., N/m^2), compound units, or domain-specific units outside the 5 supported domains.

(2) Heuristic reranking: the merge stage uses a hand-tuned composite score rather than a learned reranker (cross-encoder or ColBERT). This limits the system's ability to optimally weight branches.

(3) Regex-based extraction: measurement and formula extraction uses regex patterns rather than NLP-based extraction (like CQE's dependency parsing). This may miss complex measurement expressions.

(4) No HPC-scale testing: evaluation uses a constructed benchmark corpus, not a petabyte-scale HPC deployment. Performance characteristics at HPC scale (millions of HDF5 files, distributed storage) remain untested.</action>
