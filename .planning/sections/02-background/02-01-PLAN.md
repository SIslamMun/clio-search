# Plan 02-01: Hybrid Retrieval Foundations + Numeracy Crisis + Quantity-Aware Retrieval

## Context
This plan covers the first half of the Background and Related Work section. It positions our work against three areas: (1) hybrid retrieval foundations that we build upon, (2) the numeracy crisis that motivates why numbers are fundamentally broken in retrieval/LLMs, and (3) quantity-aware retrieval systems that attempt to address the problem but fall short of arithmetic conversion. Ends with a clear gap statement.

  <action>Write ~175 words establishing that numbers are fundamentally broken in retrieval and LLMs. Key evidence: Numeracy Gap (Deng et al., EACL 2026): 0.54 binary retrieval accuracy across 13 embedding models on numerical content -- near random chance. Cite GSM-Symbolic (Apple, ICLR 2025): 65% accuracy drop when numeric values change in math word problems.
