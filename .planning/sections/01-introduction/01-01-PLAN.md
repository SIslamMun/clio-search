# Plan 01-01: AI Agents Entering Science + Three Compounding Failures

## Context
This plan covers the opening hook and problem statement for the paper. It establishes that AI agents are performing real science but fail at data retrieval, then identifies three compounding failures that explain why: dimensional blindness, formula opacity, and storage fragmentation. Ends with the motivating example that threads through the entire paper.
