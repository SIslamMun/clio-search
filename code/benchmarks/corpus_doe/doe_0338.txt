Title: Radiosondes Corrected for Inaccuracy in RH Measurements
Authors: ['Miloshevich, Larry [ORNL]']
Date: 2006-01-09T04:00:00Z
DOI: https://doi.org/10.5439/1659429
Organization: ['Atmospheric Radiation Measurement (ARM) Archive, Oak Ridge National Laboratory (ORNL), Oak Ridge, TN (US)', 'ARM Data Center, Oak Ridge National Laboratory (ORNL), Oak Ridge, TN (United States)']
Subjects: ['54 ENVIRONMENTAL SCIENCES', 'atmospheric moisture', 'atmospheric pressure', 'atmospheric temperature', 'radiosonde corrections', 'relative humidity']

Corrections for inaccuracy in Vaisala radiosonde RH measurements have been applied to ARM SGP radiosonde soundings ('sgpsonde') for the time period September 2000 to December 2005. This document describes the corrections and their impact on the data, and gives references with more detailed information.
