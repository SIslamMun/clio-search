This dataset includes atmospheric measurements obtained with the Black Swift Technologies S0 uncrewed aircraft system at the ARM SGP facility and a nearby mesonet site (Marshall). &nbsp;Flights were conducted over a one-week period, alongside platforms deployed by the University of Nebraska - Lincoln.
