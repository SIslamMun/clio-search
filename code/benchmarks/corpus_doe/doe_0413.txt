Title: PA-BCI Meteorological dataset 1985-2018
Authors: ['Faybishenko, Boris [LBNL; Lawrence Berkeley National Lab]', 'Paton, Steve [Smithsonian Tropical Research Institution]']
Date: 2021-01-01T04:00:00Z

Organization: ['Next-Generation Ecosystem Experiments Tropics', 'Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory', 'Smithsonian Tropical Research Institute']
Subjects: ['54 ENVIRONMENTAL SCIENCES']

This data package contains a QA/QC-ed meteorological time series dataset from 1985 to 2018, including relative humidity, temperature, solar radiation, rainfall, wind speed, and barometric pressure at Barro Colorado Island (PA-BCI) in Panama. The data were collected at the elevations of 42 m and 48 m at the BCI Lutz station, except for rainfall that was collected at the BCI Clearing station at the elevation of 1 m. Individual time series datasets were collected using electro-mechanical (electronic sensors, data loggers, chart recorders, etc.). The rainfall dataset was collected using the tipping bucket method, and the data could have been underestimated by 13-17% (likely the large values). The data is included as a single CSV file.
