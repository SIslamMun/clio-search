Title: Surface Meteorological Data from Sequim "Upstairs" PNNL Campus
Authors: ['Goldberger, Lexie A [PNNL]']
Date: 2023-07-26T04:00:00Z
DOI: https://doi.org/10.25584/1997410
Organization: ['Pacific Northwest National Laboratory 2']
Subjects: ['meteorological, meteorological data, Meteorological Measurements, air temperature, surface wind speeds']

Surface meteorological data (3m wind speed, wind direction, 2m air temperature, relative humidity, pressure, downwelling irradiance, total rainfall) gathered on the Sequim-PNNL campus ('upstairs'). Equipment is manufactured and calibrated by Campbell Scientific. Placement and height of sensors follows recommended guidelines of the NWS.

Source: DOE Data Explorer (OSTI). U.S. Department of Energy scientific dataset.
