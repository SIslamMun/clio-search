The following folders contain 3 friction experiments on Utah FORGE core material from depths of 6735 - 8533ft. The experiment conditions range from 100% relative humidity at room temperature to fluid-saturated at 110C. Experiments were done on both intact samples in a single-direct shear configuration, double-direct shear, and gouge.
