Satellite-based retrievals of cloud and radiation properties are available in this value-added product provided by Bill Smith’s group at NASA/Langley using the VISST (Visible Infrared Solar-Infrared Split Window Technique) algorithm.  This product contains VISST-derived pixel-level products from the GOES-16 satellite, rectilinear projection 2-D array.
