Title: Site G - Atmospheric Sensor / Reviewed Data

This dataset contains meteorological measurements including temperature, pressure, and relative humidity at multiple heights deployed on tethered balloon system at AWAKEN site G. Z05 refers to the fifth height position of the iMet-XQ2 mounted on the tethered balloon.

Source: DOE Data Explorer (OSTI). U.S. Department of Energy scientific dataset.
