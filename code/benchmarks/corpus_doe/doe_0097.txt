Precipitation, Net radiation, and PAR data files (1998, rings 4 and 5):First four columns show precipitation (mm) and net radiation (W/m^2).Ring 4 measures overstory precipitation (po), and ring 5 measures throughfall (pt).The last data column shows PAR (millimol/m^2*s).Air temperature, Relative Humidity, SVP, and VPD (r1 - r6):Data columns are organised by ring number.For each ring there is:- Temperature (Degrees C)- Relative humidity (absolute values)- SVP (Saturated Vapor Pressure - kPa)- VPD (Vapor Pressure Deficit - kPa)measurements are done at 2/3 of canopy heightNote: gap-filled data are available for some variables and years.
