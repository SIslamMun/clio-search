Particle number size distributions (10 to 400 nm) were measured with BNL&#39;s Fast Integrating Mobility Spectrometer onboard the G1 research aircraft during HISCALE campaign. Size bin margins are listed in &#39;HISCALE_FIMS_bins_R1.dat&#39;. For stp conversion ambient pressure and temperature are added to the data set.
