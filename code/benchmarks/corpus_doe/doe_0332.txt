<p>The following dataset relates to the TAMU TRACER radiosonde IOP, which involved launching iMet-4 and iMet-4C radiosonde models from various locations in southeast Texas. The radiosondes were launched between June and September 2022 during the TRACER IOP. Telemetry data for all IOPs were copied using the iMet-OS II software provided by radiosonde manufacturer, Intermet Systems.&nbsp;</p>
