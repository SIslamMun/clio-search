Title: Site A1 - Surface Meteorological Station / Reviewed Data

Date: 2025-06-24T04:00:00Z

Subjects: ['17 WIND ENERGY']

This dataset contains meteorological data both from the ground and tether termination near the balloon on tethered balloon system at AWAKEN site A1. The met data include time stamp, wind speed, temperature, relative humidity, and pressure.

Source: DOE Data Explorer (OSTI). U.S. Department of Energy scientific dataset.
