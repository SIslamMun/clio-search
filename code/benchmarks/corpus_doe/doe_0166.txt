These data were collected a DJI M600-P hexcopter UAS operated by the University of Nebraska-Lincoln.&nbsp; The M600-P includes housings, described by Islam et al. (2019), for the temperature/humidity sensors.&nbsp; Data were collected via profiles to ~120 m AGL along with loiters near 60 m AGL.&nbsp;&nbsp;

Source: DOE Data Explorer (OSTI). U.S. Department of Energy scientific dataset.
