Title: West Flank Coso Downhole Temperature Data
Authors: ['Blankenship, Doug [Sandia National Laboratories]']
Date: 2008-03-01T04:00:00Z
DOI: https://doi.org/10.15121/1287544
Organization: ['DOE Geothermal Data Repository', 'Sandia National Laboratories']
Subjects: ['15 GEOTHERMAL ENERGY', 'CA', 'California', 'EGS', 'FORGE', 'Temperature Logs', 'West Flank', 'coso', 'geothermal', 'pressure']

Downhole temperature data for the three wells inside the West Flank FORGE footprint; 83-11, TCH 74-2 and TCH 48-11. TCH 74-2 and TCH 48-11 were both collected before 1990 and 83-11 was collected in 2009. The are compiled into one spreadsheet for ease of visualization. Plot of data included.

Source: DOE Data Explorer (OSTI). U.S. Department of Energy scientific dataset.
