The Interagency Working Group for Airborne Data and Telemetry Systems (IWGADTS) is developing a suite of tools to promote standardization of instrument interface, data format and data processing. ARM Arial Facility uses the IWGADTS (&quot;IWG1&quot;) format and the detailed information can be found at: http://www.eol.ucar.edu/iwgadts.
