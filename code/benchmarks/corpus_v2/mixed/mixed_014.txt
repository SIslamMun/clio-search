Biomedical Device Testing: Blood Pump Characterization, Unit BP-314

A centrifugal blood pump was tested on a mock circulatory loop to characterize
hydrodynamic performance and hemolysis.

Test loop configuration:
- Tubing ID: 8 mm (0.008 m)
- Reservoir volume: 1.3 L
- Working fluid: bovine blood
- Temperature: 36.9 degC
- Viscosity: 2.7 cP

Pump performance at 2574 rpm:
- Flow rate: 7.2 L/min
- Pressure rise: 73 mmHg (14.5 kPa)
- Outlet pressure: 108 mmHg (16.8 kPa)
- Flow velocity at outlet: 0.7 m/s
- Maximum shear stress: 84 Pa

Hemolysis assessment:
- Test duration: 2 h (14503 s)
- Plasma-free hemoglobin increase: 6 mg/dL
- Normalized index of hemolysis: 0.0295 g/100L

The pump housing wall thickness was 3.2 mm (polycarbonate, sterilizable).
Mass of pump head: 336 g. Bearing gap: 0.19 mm.
Reynolds number in the impeller passage: approximately 41149.
