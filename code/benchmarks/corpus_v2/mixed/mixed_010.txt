Biomedical Device Testing: Blood Pump Characterization, Unit BP-310

A centrifugal blood pump was tested on a mock circulatory loop to characterize
hydrodynamic performance and hemolysis.

Test loop configuration:
- Tubing ID: 6 mm (0.011 m)
- Reservoir volume: 1.3 L
- Working fluid: porcine blood
- Temperature: 37.5 degC
- Viscosity: 4.3 cP

Pump performance at 2834 rpm:
- Flow rate: 4.3 L/min
- Pressure rise: 117 mmHg (13.2 kPa)
- Outlet pressure: 101 mmHg (16.0 kPa)
- Flow velocity at outlet: 1.8 m/s
- Maximum shear stress: 339 Pa

Hemolysis assessment:
- Test duration: 4 h (18086 s)
- Plasma-free hemoglobin increase: 31 mg/dL
- Normalized index of hemolysis: 0.0353 g/100L

The pump housing wall thickness was 2.2 mm (polycarbonate, sterilizable).
Mass of pump head: 471 g. Bearing gap: 0.07 mm.
Reynolds number in the impeller passage: approximately 49949.
