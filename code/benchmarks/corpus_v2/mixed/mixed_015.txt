Geotechnical Site Investigation: Borehole BH-415

Location: Wind farm foundation
Depth: 31.7 m below ground level

Soil profile:
- 0-5.0 m: Fill material, unit weight 17 kN/m^3
- 4.3-10.6 m: Medium clay
- 14.2-31.8 m: Weathered rock
- Below 22.7 m: Hardpan

In-situ testing:
- SPT N-values: 10 (soft layer) to 41 (dense layer)
- CPT tip resistance: 12.2 MPa (15314 kPa)
- Pore water pressure at 12 m: 135 kPa (130654 Pa)
- Groundwater table: 5.3 m below surface

Laboratory tests:
- Unconfined compressive strength: 50 kPa
- Consolidation: Cc = 0.46, preconsolidation pressure 278 kPa
- Permeability: 1.8e-06 m/s (9.487 mm/s)

The effective overburden pressure at 17 m depth is approximately
294 kPa. Foundation design: mat foundation, bearing capacity
494 kPa (allowable). Settlement estimate: 54 mm
over 14 years. Sample mass extracted: 3.8 kg.
