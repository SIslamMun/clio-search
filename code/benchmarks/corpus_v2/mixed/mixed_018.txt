Biomedical Device Testing: Blood Pump Characterization, Unit BP-318

A centrifugal blood pump was tested on a mock circulatory loop to characterize
hydrodynamic performance and hemolysis.

Test loop configuration:
- Tubing ID: 9 mm (0.010 m)
- Reservoir volume: 1.5 L
- Working fluid: glycerol-water (40%)
- Temperature: 35.1 degC
- Viscosity: 2.6 cP

Pump performance at 3174 rpm:
- Flow rate: 5.6 L/min
- Pressure rise: 72 mmHg (14.2 kPa)
- Outlet pressure: 127 mmHg (13.9 kPa)
- Flow velocity at outlet: 0.9 m/s
- Maximum shear stress: 465 Pa

Hemolysis assessment:
- Test duration: 4 h (13039 s)
- Plasma-free hemoglobin increase: 49 mg/dL
- Normalized index of hemolysis: 0.0322 g/100L

The pump housing wall thickness was 4.6 mm (polycarbonate, sterilizable).
Mass of pump head: 391 g. Bearing gap: 0.17 mm.
Reynolds number in the impeller passage: approximately 10619.
