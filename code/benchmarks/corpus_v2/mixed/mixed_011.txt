Geotechnical Site Investigation: Borehole BH-411

Location: Wind farm foundation
Depth: 34.3 m below ground level

Soil profile:
- 0-2.8 m: Fill material, unit weight 19 kN/m^3
- 3.1-13.8 m: Soft clay
- 11.3-30.7 m: Gravel
- Below 34.9 m: Hardpan

In-situ testing:
- SPT N-values: 6 (soft layer) to 48 (dense layer)
- CPT tip resistance: 9.3 MPa (26666 kPa)
- Pore water pressure at 15 m: 137 kPa (114794 Pa)
- Groundwater table: 7.7 m below surface

Laboratory tests:
- Unconfined compressive strength: 22 kPa
- Consolidation: Cc = 0.71, preconsolidation pressure 157 kPa
- Permeability: 4.2e-07 m/s (5.728 mm/s)

The effective overburden pressure at 14 m depth is approximately
210 kPa. Foundation design: mat foundation, bearing capacity
234 kPa (allowable). Settlement estimate: 29 mm
over 24 years. Sample mass extracted: 7.8 kg.
