Geotechnical Site Investigation: Borehole BH-403

Location: Seaport expansion
Depth: 49.2 m below ground level

Soil profile:
- 0-4.7 m: Fill material, unit weight 18 kN/m^3
- 2.8-14.7 m: Stiff clay
- 11.2-22.7 m: Stiff glacial till
- Below 32.1 m: Hardpan

In-situ testing:
- SPT N-values: 10 (soft layer) to 68 (dense layer)
- CPT tip resistance: 25.4 MPa (16660 kPa)
- Pore water pressure at 8 m: 124 kPa (105358 Pa)
- Groundwater table: 2.5 m below surface

Laboratory tests:
- Unconfined compressive strength: 58 kPa
- Consolidation: Cc = 0.60, preconsolidation pressure 375 kPa
- Permeability: 9.2e-06 m/s (1.328 mm/s)

The effective overburden pressure at 17 m depth is approximately
295 kPa. Foundation design: mat foundation, bearing capacity
271 kPa (allowable). Settlement estimate: 59 mm
over 21 years. Sample mass extracted: 7.7 kg.
