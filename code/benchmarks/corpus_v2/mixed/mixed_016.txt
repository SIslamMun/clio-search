Wind Energy Assessment: Turbine Site 116

This multi-disciplinary report covers the aerodynamic, structural, and atmospheric
analysis for a proposed wind turbine installation.

Atmospheric conditions (annual averages):
- Mean wind speed at hub height (80 m): 8.4 m/s (19.6 km/h)
- Wind power density: 422 W/m^2
- Extreme gust (50-year return): 41 m/s
- Barometric pressure (site elevation 493 m): 99.1 kPa
- Air density: 1.090 kg/m^3

Turbine specifications:
- Rotor diameter: 122 m (120918 mm)
- Blade length: 40 m
- Blade root thickness: 79 mm
- Tower height: 79 m
- Rated wind speed: 13.5 m/s
- Cut-in speed: 4 m/s

Structural analysis:
- Maximum blade root bending moment: 25.7 MN*m
- Blade tip deflection: 7.2 m (4317 mm)
- Tower base von Mises stress: 182 MPa (126077 kPa)
- Foundation design pressure: 396 kPa on soil

The power output P = 0.5 * rho * A * Cp * v^3 at rated conditions gives
approximately 4.2 MW. Annual energy production estimate:
18 GWh at a capacity factor of 46%.
Fatigue life assessment: 20+ years at 5.2e+08 cycles.
