Biomedical Device Testing: Blood Pump Characterization, Unit BP-306

A centrifugal blood pump was tested on a mock circulatory loop to characterize
hydrodynamic performance and hemolysis.

Test loop configuration:
- Tubing ID: 9 mm (0.007 m)
- Reservoir volume: 1.5 L
- Working fluid: porcine blood
- Temperature: 36.9 degC
- Viscosity: 4.0 cP

Pump performance at 3119 rpm:
- Flow rate: 6.4 L/min
- Pressure rise: 78 mmHg (16.7 kPa)
- Outlet pressure: 106 mmHg (15.1 kPa)
- Flow velocity at outlet: 3.0 m/s
- Maximum shear stress: 84 Pa

Hemolysis assessment:
- Test duration: 6 h (17785 s)
- Plasma-free hemoglobin increase: 10 mg/dL
- Normalized index of hemolysis: 0.0030 g/100L

The pump housing wall thickness was 2.8 mm (polycarbonate, sterilizable).
Mass of pump head: 206 g. Bearing gap: 0.07 mm.
Reynolds number in the impeller passage: approximately 21712.
