Biomedical Device Testing: Blood Pump Characterization, Unit BP-302

A centrifugal blood pump was tested on a mock circulatory loop to characterize
hydrodynamic performance and hemolysis.

Test loop configuration:
- Tubing ID: 11 mm (0.009 m)
- Reservoir volume: 1.5 L
- Working fluid: porcine blood
- Temperature: 35.4 degC
- Viscosity: 3.5 cP

Pump performance at 3086 rpm:
- Flow rate: 6.4 L/min
- Pressure rise: 110 mmHg (13.2 kPa)
- Outlet pressure: 118 mmHg (17.0 kPa)
- Flow velocity at outlet: 2.5 m/s
- Maximum shear stress: 460 Pa

Hemolysis assessment:
- Test duration: 5 h (9701 s)
- Plasma-free hemoglobin increase: 42 mg/dL
- Normalized index of hemolysis: 0.0061 g/100L

The pump housing wall thickness was 4.0 mm (polycarbonate, sterilizable).
Mass of pump head: 342 g. Bearing gap: 0.10 mm.
Reynolds number in the impeller passage: approximately 30209.
