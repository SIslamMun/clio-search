Aerospace Component Test: Thermal-Structural Qualification, Test 217

Component: Combustion liner
Material: C/SiC composite

Thermal loading:
- Gas temperature: 1678 degC
- Heat flux: 3.9 MW/m^2
- Cooling fluid: water
- Coolant pressure: 15 MPa (14981 kPa)
- Coolant flow velocity: 32 m/s (99 km/h)

Structural measurements:
- Peak thermal stress: 283 MPa
- Membrane stress: 91 MPa
- Wall thickness: 1.7 mm
- Displacement at tip: 3.4 mm
- Component mass: 12.2 kg (10971 g)

Pressure measurements:
- Chamber pressure: 9 MPa
- Nozzle exit pressure: 75 kPa (20768 Pa)
- External ambient: 23.3 kPa

Test duration: 492 s (7.3 min). The component
survived 31 thermal cycles without cracking. Post-test inspection
by DPI and X-ray revealed no defects exceeding the acceptance criteria of
0.7 mm indication length.
