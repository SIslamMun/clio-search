- Heat flux: 8.7 MW/m^2
- Cooling fluid: LN2
- Coolant pressure: 23 MPa (17277 kPa)
- Coolant flow velocity: 16 m/s (145 km/h)

Structural measurements:
- Peak thermal stress: 679 MPa
- Membrane stress: 122 MPa
- Wall thickness: 7.0 mm
- Displacement at tip: 4.7 mm
- Component mass: 20.6 kg (47922 g)

Pressure measurements:
- Chamber pressure: 20 MPa
- Nozzle exit pressure: 38 kPa (60243 Pa)
- External ambient: 1.4 kPa

Test duration: 173 s (6.4 min). The component
survived 50 thermal cycles without cracking. Post-test inspection
by DPI and X-ray revealed no defects exceeding the acceptance criteria of
