Wind Energy Assessment: Turbine Site 104

This multi-disciplinary report covers the aerodynamic, structural, and atmospheric
analysis for a proposed wind turbine installation.

Atmospheric conditions (annual averages):
- Mean wind speed at hub height (80 m): 10.3 m/s (32.2 km/h)
- Wind power density: 550 W/m^2
- Extreme gust (50-year return): 68 m/s
- Barometric pressure (site elevation 1449 m): 94.2 kPa
- Air density: 1.223 kg/m^3

Turbine specifications:
- Rotor diameter: 128 m (158973 mm)
- Blade length: 57 m
- Blade root thickness: 41 mm
- Tower height: 66 m
- Rated wind speed: 11.9 m/s
- Cut-in speed: 3 m/s

Structural analysis:
- Maximum blade root bending moment: 6.4 MN*m
- Blade tip deflection: 4.3 m (6928 mm)
- Tower base von Mises stress: 248 MPa (202509 kPa)
- Foundation design pressure: 244 kPa on soil

The power output P = 0.5 * rho * A * Cp * v^3 at rated conditions gives
approximately 2.4 MW. Annual energy production estimate:
21 GWh at a capacity factor of 30%.
Fatigue life assessment: 20+ years at 5.5e+08 cycles.
