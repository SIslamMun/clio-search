Geotechnical Site Investigation: Borehole BH-419

Location: Highway bridge abutment
Depth: 28.0 m below ground level

Soil profile:
- 0-5.0 m: Fill material, unit weight 19 kN/m^3
- 2.5-13.8 m: Medium clay
- 13.6-36.4 m: Gravel
- Below 32.5 m: Hardpan

In-situ testing:
- SPT N-values: 6 (soft layer) to 51 (dense layer)
- CPT tip resistance: 21.9 MPa (10609 kPa)
- Pore water pressure at 10 m: 109 kPa (72981 Pa)
- Groundwater table: 3.7 m below surface

Laboratory tests:
- Unconfined compressive strength: 40 kPa
- Consolidation: Cc = 0.24, preconsolidation pressure 444 kPa
- Permeability: 5.0e-06 m/s (4.199 mm/s)

The effective overburden pressure at 11 m depth is approximately
119 kPa. Foundation design: mat foundation, bearing capacity
390 kPa (allowable). Settlement estimate: 65 mm
over 6 years. Sample mass extracted: 8.3 kg.
