Geotechnical Site Investigation: Borehole BH-407

Location: Downtown construction site
Depth: 23.2 m below ground level

Soil profile:
- 0-4.1 m: Fill material, unit weight 16 kN/m^3
- 2.5-10.3 m: Soft clay
- 13.4-32.5 m: Weathered rock
- Below 29.8 m: Hardpan

In-situ testing:
- SPT N-values: 10 (soft layer) to 75 (dense layer)
- CPT tip resistance: 2.6 MPa (21377 kPa)
- Pore water pressure at 10 m: 146 kPa (102061 Pa)
- Groundwater table: 6.5 m below surface

Laboratory tests:
- Unconfined compressive strength: 49 kPa
- Consolidation: Cc = 0.48, preconsolidation pressure 261 kPa
- Permeability: 3.5e-06 m/s (8.834 mm/s)

The effective overburden pressure at 17 m depth is approximately
166 kPa. Foundation design: mat foundation, bearing capacity
317 kPa (allowable). Settlement estimate: 96 mm
over 20 years. Sample mass extracted: 5.8 kg.
