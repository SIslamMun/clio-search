Wind Energy Assessment: Turbine Site 112

This multi-disciplinary report covers the aerodynamic, structural, and atmospheric
analysis for a proposed wind turbine installation.

Atmospheric conditions (annual averages):
- Mean wind speed at hub height (80 m): 6.5 m/s (40.8 km/h)
- Wind power density: 711 W/m^2
- Extreme gust (50-year return): 44 m/s
- Barometric pressure (site elevation 450 m): 93.0 kPa
- Air density: 1.063 kg/m^3

Turbine specifications:
- Rotor diameter: 90 m (140388 mm)
- Blade length: 64 m
- Blade root thickness: 42 mm
- Tower height: 105 m
- Rated wind speed: 15.0 m/s
- Cut-in speed: 4 m/s

Structural analysis:
- Maximum blade root bending moment: 21.1 MN*m
- Blade tip deflection: 3.4 m (6358 mm)
- Tower base von Mises stress: 138 MPa (129613 kPa)
- Foundation design pressure: 374 kPa on soil

The power output P = 0.5 * rho * A * Cp * v^3 at rated conditions gives
approximately 7.7 MW. Annual energy production estimate:
12 GWh at a capacity factor of 42%.
Fatigue life assessment: 20+ years at 2.4e+08 cycles.
