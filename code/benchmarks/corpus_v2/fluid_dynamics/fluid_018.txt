CFD Validation Study: Backward-Facing Step, Case BFS-4018

A RANS simulation of flow over a backward-facing step was performed to validate
the k-epsilon RNG turbulence model.
The step height was h = 32 mm and the expansion ratio was
2.00.

Inlet conditions:
- Freestream velocity: 17.0 m/s
- Turbulence intensity: 2.2%
- Reynolds number (based on step height): Re_h = 27699

Computational details:
- Domain: 29h upstream, 61h downstream
- Mesh: structured, 1.9 million cells
- Near-wall spacing: y+ < 3.5
- Time step: 0.0044 s (for URANS)

Results:
- Reattachment length: 7.0h (experiment: 6.8h)
- Peak reversed flow velocity: -0.25 times U_inlet
- Pressure coefficient at reattachment: Cp = 0.197
- Wall pressure recovery: 89% of theoretical

The static pressure upstream of the step was 127.9 kPa while
the base pressure (immediately behind step) dropped to 96.5 kPa.
This pressure difference of 9473 Pa drives the recirculation.
Simulation wall-clock time: 47 h on 213 cores.
