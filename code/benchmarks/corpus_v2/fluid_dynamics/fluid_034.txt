Wind Tunnel Test: Airfoil NACA 2412, Run WT-5034

Test section: 1.1 m x 0.7 m,
turbulence intensity < 0.23%.
Model chord: 192 mm, span: 440 mm.
End plates installed to enforce 2-D flow.

Test conditions:
- Freestream velocity: 44.2 m/s (104 km/h)
- Dynamic pressure: 783 Pa (q = 0.5 * rho * V^2)
- Reynolds number: 1.8 x 10^6
- Angle of attack sweep: -4 to +20 deg in 2-deg increments
- Stagnation pressure: 102.9 kPa

Key results at alpha = 11 deg:
- Lift coefficient: Cl = 1.150
- Drag coefficient: Cd = 0.0243
- Pitching moment: Cm = -0.1084
- Stall angle: approximately 13 deg

Surface pressure distribution was measured using 72 pressure taps
connected to a Scanivalve system sampling at 523 Hz. The minimum
surface pressure (suction peak) was -2886 Pa gauge, occurring
at x/c = 0.132. Wake survey at x/c = 2 confirmed the drag
measurement within 6% uncertainty.
