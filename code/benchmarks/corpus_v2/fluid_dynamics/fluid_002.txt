Open Channel Flow Measurement: Site OC-3002

A rectangular open channel with width 0.53 m and bed slope
0.0074 was instrumented for velocity profiling. The channel
lining is smooth concrete (Manning's n = 0.0148).

Measured conditions at the test cross-section:
- Water depth: 115 mm (0.181 m)
- Mean velocity: 1.64 m/s
- Flow rate: 444 L/s
- Froude number: Fr = 0.68
- Reynolds number: Re = 172569

Velocity was measured using an acoustic Doppler velocimeter (ADV) at
100 Hz. The vertical velocity profile was sampled at
22 points from 15 mm above the bed to the
free surface. Maximum velocity of 1.54 m/s occurred at
approximately 0.6 of the flow depth.

The hydraulic gradient corresponds to a pressure difference of 389 Pa
per meter of channel length. The boundary shear stress was 4.6 Pa.
A hydraulic jump was observed at 5.5 m downstream with a conjugate
depth ratio of 3.5. Upstream Froude number: 2.7.
Downstream velocity: 0.47 m/s (1.1 km/h).
