CFD Validation Study: Backward-Facing Step, Case BFS-4013

A RANS simulation of flow over a backward-facing step was performed to validate
the Spalart-Allmaras turbulence model.
The step height was h = 30 mm and the expansion ratio was
1.18.

Inlet conditions:
- Freestream velocity: 14.8 m/s
- Turbulence intensity: 2.6%
- Reynolds number (based on step height): Re_h = 36552

Computational details:
- Domain: 24h upstream, 54h downstream
- Mesh: structured, 0.6 million cells
- Near-wall spacing: y+ < 2.7
- Time step: 0.0044 s (for URANS)

Results:
- Reattachment length: 7.0h (experiment: 6.5h)
- Peak reversed flow velocity: -0.19 times U_inlet
- Pressure coefficient at reattachment: Cp = 0.269
- Wall pressure recovery: 73% of theoretical

The static pressure upstream of the step was 188.2 kPa while
the base pressure (immediately behind step) dropped to 98.6 kPa.
This pressure difference of 7875 Pa drives the recirculation.
Simulation wall-clock time: 19 h on 118 cores.
