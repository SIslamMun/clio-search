Wind Tunnel Test: Airfoil NACA 4415, Run WT-5019

Test section: 1.6 m x 1.2 m,
turbulence intensity < 0.30%.
Model chord: 222 mm, span: 468 mm.
End plates installed to enforce 2-D flow.

Test conditions:
- Freestream velocity: 57.1 m/s (171 km/h)
- Dynamic pressure: 647 Pa (q = 0.5 * rho * V^2)
- Reynolds number: 1.2 x 10^6
- Angle of attack sweep: -4 to +20 deg in 2-deg increments
- Stagnation pressure: 103.2 kPa

Key results at alpha = 11 deg:
- Lift coefficient: Cl = 0.855
- Drag coefficient: Cd = 0.0143
- Pitching moment: Cm = -0.0495
- Stall angle: approximately 13 deg

Surface pressure distribution was measured using 37 pressure taps
connected to a Scanivalve system sampling at 394 Hz. The minimum
surface pressure (suction peak) was -612 Pa gauge, occurring
at x/c = 0.030. Wake survey at x/c = 2 confirmed the drag
measurement within 8% uncertainty.
