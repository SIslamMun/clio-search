Open Channel Flow Measurement: Site OC-3012

A rectangular open channel with width 1.59 m and bed slope
0.0056 was instrumented for velocity profiling. The channel
lining is smooth concrete (Manning's n = 0.0114).

Measured conditions at the test cross-section:
- Water depth: 197 mm (0.149 m)
- Mean velocity: 1.06 m/s
- Flow rate: 79 L/s
- Froude number: Fr = 0.36
- Reynolds number: Re = 211405

Velocity was measured using an acoustic Doppler velocimeter (ADV) at
100 Hz. The vertical velocity profile was sampled at
23 points from 7 mm above the bed to the
free surface. Maximum velocity of 2.22 m/s occurred at
approximately 0.5 of the flow depth.

The hydraulic gradient corresponds to a pressure difference of 197 Pa
per meter of channel length. The boundary shear stress was 12.0 Pa.
A hydraulic jump was observed at 5.2 m downstream with a conjugate
depth ratio of 3.4. Upstream Froude number: 2.0.
Downstream velocity: 0.61 m/s (2.2 km/h).
