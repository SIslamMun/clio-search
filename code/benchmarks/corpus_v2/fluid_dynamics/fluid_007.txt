Open Channel Flow Measurement: Site OC-3007

A rectangular open channel with width 1.68 m and bed slope
0.0097 was instrumented for velocity profiling. The channel
lining is smooth concrete (Manning's n = 0.0149).

Measured conditions at the test cross-section:
- Water depth: 367 mm (0.384 m)
- Mean velocity: 1.23 m/s
- Flow rate: 152 L/s
- Froude number: Fr = 0.66
- Reynolds number: Re = 92578

Velocity was measured using an acoustic Doppler velocimeter (ADV) at
100 Hz. The vertical velocity profile was sampled at
30 points from 7 mm above the bed to the
free surface. Maximum velocity of 1.16 m/s occurred at
approximately 0.6 of the flow depth.

The hydraulic gradient corresponds to a pressure difference of 493 Pa
per meter of channel length. The boundary shear stress was 16.0 Pa.
A hydraulic jump was observed at 4.7 m downstream with a conjugate
depth ratio of 2.7. Upstream Froude number: 1.8.
Downstream velocity: 0.66 m/s (2.1 km/h).
