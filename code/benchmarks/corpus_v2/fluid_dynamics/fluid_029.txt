Wind Tunnel Test: Airfoil NACA 0012, Run WT-5029

Test section: 1.6 m x 1.5 m,
turbulence intensity < 0.29%.
Model chord: 258 mm, span: 427 mm.
End plates installed to enforce 2-D flow.

Test conditions:
- Freestream velocity: 32.1 m/s (91 km/h)
- Dynamic pressure: 975 Pa (q = 0.5 * rho * V^2)
- Reynolds number: 0.5 x 10^6
- Angle of attack sweep: -4 to +20 deg in 2-deg increments
- Stagnation pressure: 101.8 kPa

Key results at alpha = 11 deg:
- Lift coefficient: Cl = 1.189
- Drag coefficient: Cd = 0.0068
- Pitching moment: Cm = -0.0642
- Stall angle: approximately 16 deg

Surface pressure distribution was measured using 34 pressure taps
connected to a Scanivalve system sampling at 346 Hz. The minimum
surface pressure (suction peak) was -2485 Pa gauge, occurring
at x/c = 0.033. Wake survey at x/c = 2 confirmed the drag
measurement within 2% uncertainty.
