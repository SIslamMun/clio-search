Wind Tunnel Test: Airfoil NACA 6412, Run WT-5039

Test section: 1.3 m x 0.6 m,
turbulence intensity < 0.10%.
Model chord: 176 mm, span: 450 mm.
End plates installed to enforce 2-D flow.

Test conditions:
- Freestream velocity: 34.5 m/s (181 km/h)
- Dynamic pressure: 1175 Pa (q = 0.5 * rho * V^2)
- Reynolds number: 1.7 x 10^6
- Angle of attack sweep: -4 to +20 deg in 2-deg increments
- Stagnation pressure: 101.4 kPa

Key results at alpha = 9 deg:
- Lift coefficient: Cl = 0.538
- Drag coefficient: Cd = 0.0111
- Pitching moment: Cm = -0.0371
- Stall angle: approximately 17 deg

Surface pressure distribution was measured using 92 pressure taps
connected to a Scanivalve system sampling at 377 Hz. The minimum
surface pressure (suction peak) was -2884 Pa gauge, occurring
at x/c = 0.030. Wake survey at x/c = 2 confirmed the drag
measurement within 8% uncertainty.
