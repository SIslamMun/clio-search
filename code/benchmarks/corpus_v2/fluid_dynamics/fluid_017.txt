Open Channel Flow Measurement: Site OC-3017

A rectangular open channel with width 0.59 m and bed slope
0.0067 was instrumented for velocity profiling. The channel
lining is smooth concrete (Manning's n = 0.0135).

Measured conditions at the test cross-section:
- Water depth: 436 mm (0.159 m)
- Mean velocity: 1.46 m/s
- Flow rate: 25 L/s
- Froude number: Fr = 1.44
- Reynolds number: Re = 67616

Velocity was measured using an acoustic Doppler velocimeter (ADV) at
25 Hz. The vertical velocity profile was sampled at
10 points from 9 mm above the bed to the
free surface. Maximum velocity of 1.28 m/s occurred at
approximately 0.6 of the flow depth.

The hydraulic gradient corresponds to a pressure difference of 142 Pa
per meter of channel length. The boundary shear stress was 10.5 Pa.
A hydraulic jump was observed at 7.3 m downstream with a conjugate
depth ratio of 2.7. Upstream Froude number: 2.7.
Downstream velocity: 0.78 m/s (2.8 km/h).
