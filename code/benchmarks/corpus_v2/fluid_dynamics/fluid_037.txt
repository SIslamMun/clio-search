Open Channel Flow Measurement: Site OC-3037

A rectangular open channel with width 0.56 m and bed slope
0.0030 was instrumented for velocity profiling. The channel
lining is smooth concrete (Manning's n = 0.0128).

Measured conditions at the test cross-section:
- Water depth: 124 mm (0.355 m)
- Mean velocity: 1.89 m/s
- Flow rate: 457 L/s
- Froude number: Fr = 1.09
- Reynolds number: Re = 18247

Velocity was measured using an acoustic Doppler velocimeter (ADV) at
25 Hz. The vertical velocity profile was sampled at
27 points from 11 mm above the bed to the
free surface. Maximum velocity of 0.42 m/s occurred at
approximately 0.7 of the flow depth.

The hydraulic gradient corresponds to a pressure difference of 174 Pa
per meter of channel length. The boundary shear stress was 19.6 Pa.
A hydraulic jump was observed at 3.1 m downstream with a conjugate
depth ratio of 3.5. Upstream Froude number: 2.4.
Downstream velocity: 0.84 m/s (3.3 km/h).
