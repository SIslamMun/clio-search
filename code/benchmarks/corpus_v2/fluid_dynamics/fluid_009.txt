Wind Tunnel Test: Airfoil NACA 0012, Run WT-5009

Test section: 1.7 m x 1.0 m,
turbulence intensity < 0.22%.
Model chord: 174 mm, span: 580 mm.
End plates installed to enforce 2-D flow.

Test conditions:
- Freestream velocity: 25.1 m/s (207 km/h)
- Dynamic pressure: 1086 Pa (q = 0.5 * rho * V^2)
- Reynolds number: 0.7 x 10^6
- Angle of attack sweep: -4 to +20 deg in 2-deg increments
- Stagnation pressure: 101.3 kPa

Key results at alpha = 7 deg:
- Lift coefficient: Cl = 1.361
- Drag coefficient: Cd = 0.0126
- Pitching moment: Cm = -0.0687
- Stall angle: approximately 16 deg

Surface pressure distribution was measured using 43 pressure taps
connected to a Scanivalve system sampling at 614 Hz. The minimum
surface pressure (suction peak) was -1398 Pa gauge, occurring
at x/c = 0.097. Wake survey at x/c = 2 confirmed the drag
measurement within 5% uncertainty.
