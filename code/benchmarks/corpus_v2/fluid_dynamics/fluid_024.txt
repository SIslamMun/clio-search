Wind Tunnel Test: Airfoil NACA 0012, Run WT-5024

Test section: 0.8 m x 0.6 m,
turbulence intensity < 0.23%.
Model chord: 187 mm, span: 378 mm.
End plates installed to enforce 2-D flow.

Test conditions:
- Freestream velocity: 25.6 m/s (176 km/h)
- Dynamic pressure: 1913 Pa (q = 0.5 * rho * V^2)
- Reynolds number: 0.7 x 10^6
- Angle of attack sweep: -4 to +20 deg in 2-deg increments
- Stagnation pressure: 103.9 kPa

Key results at alpha = 6 deg:
- Lift coefficient: Cl = 1.088
- Drag coefficient: Cd = 0.0226
- Pitching moment: Cm = -0.1419
- Stall angle: approximately 13 deg

Surface pressure distribution was measured using 71 pressure taps
connected to a Scanivalve system sampling at 263 Hz. The minimum
surface pressure (suction peak) was -1986 Pa gauge, occurring
at x/c = 0.085. Wake survey at x/c = 2 confirmed the drag
measurement within 7% uncertainty.
