Open Channel Flow Measurement: Site OC-3027

A rectangular open channel with width 1.13 m and bed slope
0.0091 was instrumented for velocity profiling. The channel
lining is smooth concrete (Manning's n = 0.0128).

Measured conditions at the test cross-section:
- Water depth: 122 mm (0.153 m)
- Mean velocity: 1.01 m/s
- Flow rate: 286 L/s
- Froude number: Fr = 0.97
- Reynolds number: Re = 362724

Velocity was measured using an acoustic Doppler velocimeter (ADV) at
50 Hz. The vertical velocity profile was sampled at
10 points from 9 mm above the bed to the
free surface. Maximum velocity of 2.11 m/s occurred at
approximately 0.7 of the flow depth.

The hydraulic gradient corresponds to a pressure difference of 310 Pa
per meter of channel length. The boundary shear stress was 1.9 Pa.
A hydraulic jump was observed at 5.4 m downstream with a conjugate
depth ratio of 1.7. Upstream Froude number: 3.0.
Downstream velocity: 0.95 m/s (0.9 km/h).
