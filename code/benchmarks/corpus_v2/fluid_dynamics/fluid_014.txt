Wind Tunnel Test: Airfoil NACA 0012, Run WT-5014

Test section: 1.4 m x 0.7 m,
turbulence intensity < 0.32%.
Model chord: 161 mm, span: 451 mm.
End plates installed to enforce 2-D flow.

Test conditions:
- Freestream velocity: 49.4 m/s (99 km/h)
- Dynamic pressure: 1979 Pa (q = 0.5 * rho * V^2)
- Reynolds number: 1.4 x 10^6
- Angle of attack sweep: -4 to +20 deg in 2-deg increments
- Stagnation pressure: 101.5 kPa

Key results at alpha = 12 deg:
- Lift coefficient: Cl = 0.855
- Drag coefficient: Cd = 0.0251
- Pitching moment: Cm = -0.1059
- Stall angle: approximately 18 deg

Surface pressure distribution was measured using 128 pressure taps
connected to a Scanivalve system sampling at 247 Hz. The minimum
surface pressure (suction peak) was -2502 Pa gauge, occurring
at x/c = 0.086. Wake survey at x/c = 2 confirmed the drag
measurement within 5% uncertainty.
