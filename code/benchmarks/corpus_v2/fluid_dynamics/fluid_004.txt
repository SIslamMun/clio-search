Wind Tunnel Test: Airfoil NACA 6412, Run WT-5004

Test section: 1.4 m x 0.9 m,
turbulence intensity < 0.38%.
Model chord: 203 mm, span: 313 mm.
End plates installed to enforce 2-D flow.

Test conditions:
- Freestream velocity: 54.2 m/s (111 km/h)
- Dynamic pressure: 1996 Pa (q = 0.5 * rho * V^2)
- Reynolds number: 0.7 x 10^6
- Angle of attack sweep: -4 to +20 deg in 2-deg increments
- Stagnation pressure: 104.9 kPa

Key results at alpha = 12 deg:
- Lift coefficient: Cl = 0.568
- Drag coefficient: Cd = 0.0213
- Pitching moment: Cm = -0.1028
- Stall angle: approximately 17 deg

Surface pressure distribution was measured using 118 pressure taps
connected to a Scanivalve system sampling at 263 Hz. The minimum
surface pressure (suction peak) was -618 Pa gauge, occurring
at x/c = 0.039. Wake survey at x/c = 2 confirmed the drag
measurement within 6% uncertainty.
