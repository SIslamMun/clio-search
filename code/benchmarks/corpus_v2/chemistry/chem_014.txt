Cell ID: EC-314
Configuration: three-electrode

- Binder (PVDF): 0.58 mg
- Total electrode mass: 4.90 mg
- Active material loading: 3.8 mg/cm^2
- Electrode thickness: 50 um (0.049 mm)

Cycling conditions:

- C-rate: C/10

- Capacity retention: 96.6%
- Coulombic efficiency: 99.9%
