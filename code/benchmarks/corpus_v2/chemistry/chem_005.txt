Gas-Phase Reaction Study: 2NO2 <-> N2O4

Reactor: static reactor with volume 2.2 L
Temperature: 527 K
Total pressure: 109 kPa (67083 Pa)

Using the ideal gas law PV = nRT:
- n = PV/RT = (135 kPa * 3.2 L) / (8.314 J/(mol*K) * 418 K)
- n = 0.3440 mol

Reactant partial pressures: 28.0 kPa each, balance He
Residence time: 27.8 s
Conversion at exit: 70%

The rate constant was determined as k = 4.385e+01 cm^3/(molecule*s)
at 317 K, giving an Arrhenius activation energy
Ea = 58.5 kJ/mol from the relation k = A * e^{-Ea/RT}.
The pre-exponential factor A = 8.21e-10 cm^3/(molecule*s).

Product analysis by FTIR showed complete consumption of the limiting reagent.
Mass balance closure: 99.0%. Total reactant mass:
4.39 g. Reactor wall temperature uniformity: +/- 3 K.
