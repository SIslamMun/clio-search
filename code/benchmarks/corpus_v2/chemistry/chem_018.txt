Synthesis Report: Gold Nanoparticle Preparation

Batch ID: SYN-118
Method: Turkevich citrate reduction

Reagent preparation:
- Precursor: 50.5 mg dissolved in 53 mL deionized water
- Reducing agent: 35.2 mg in 15 mL solvent
- Stabilizer: 35.6 mg (0.005 g)

The reaction was carried out at 88 degC for 153 min
(10693 s) under continuous magnetic stirring at 369 rpm.
The pH was adjusted to 4.9 using NaOH/HCl.

After synthesis, the product was centrifuged at 5708 rpm for
18 min and washed three times with ethanol. Final product mass:
151.5 mg (yield: 45%).

Characterization:
- UV-Vis absorption peak: 525 nm
- DLS mean diameter: 89 nm
- Zeta potential: -45 mV
- XRD confirmed wurtzite crystal structure

The total reagent mass was 0.510 g. Using the Arrhenius equation
k = A * e^{-Ea/RT}, the estimated rate constant at the synthesis temperature is consistent
with literature values for Ea = 94 kJ/mol.
