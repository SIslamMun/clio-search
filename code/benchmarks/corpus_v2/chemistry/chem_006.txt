Synthesis Report: ZnO Quantum Dot Preparation

Batch ID: SYN-106
Method: sol-gel

Reagent preparation:
- Precursor: 163.7 mg dissolved in 24 mL deionized water
- Reducing agent: 90.4 mg in 22 mL solvent
- Stabilizer: 25.2 mg (0.032 g)

The reaction was carried out at 83 degC for 110 min
(10111 s) under continuous magnetic stirring at 548 rpm.
The pH was adjusted to 6.9 using NaOH/HCl.

After synthesis, the product was centrifuged at 5368 rpm for
29 min and washed three times with ethanol. Final product mass:
146.3 mg (yield: 81%).

Characterization:
- UV-Vis absorption peak: 638 nm
- DLS mean diameter: 92 nm
- Zeta potential: -46 mV
- XRD confirmed wurtzite crystal structure

The total reagent mass was 0.521 g. Using the Arrhenius equation
k = A * e^{-Ea/RT}, the estimated rate constant at the synthesis temperature is consistent
with literature values for Ea = 68 kJ/mol.
