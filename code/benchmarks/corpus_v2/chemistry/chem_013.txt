Reaction Kinetics Study: Suzuki Coupling

Experiment ID: KIN-213

Reaction conditions:
- Temperature: 55.0 degC
- Solvent: DMF
- Initial concentration: 0.111 M
- Catalyst loading: 8.5 mol%

Aliquots (1.6 mL) were withdrawn at t = 0, 57,
300, 435, 1018,
and 3270 s. Conversion was monitored by UV-Vis.

Kinetic analysis:
- Reaction order: first
- Rate constant k = 7.7788e-03 s^{-1} (at 38.0 degC)
- Half-life: 2930 s (35.7 min)
- Activation energy Ea = 68 kJ/mol (from Arrhenius plot)

The Arrhenius equation k = A * e^{-Ea/RT} with pre-exponential factor
A = 7.11e+11 s^{-1} fits the data with R^2 = 0.981.
The enthalpy of activation Delta_H_ddagger was 48 kJ/mol via
Eyring analysis. Total reaction time to 95% conversion: 34 min.
Product isolated mass: 1.43 g (1431 mg).
