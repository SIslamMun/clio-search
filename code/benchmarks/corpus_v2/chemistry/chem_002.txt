Electrochemical Characterization: Si/C Composite Electrode

Cell ID: EC-302
Configuration: pouch cell

Electrode preparation:
- Active material: 4.25 mg
- Conductive additive (Super P): 0.18 mg
- Binder (PVDF): 0.95 mg
- Total electrode mass: 6.17 mg
- Active material loading: 4.0 mg/cm^2
- Electrode thickness: 37 um (0.036 mm)

Cycling conditions:
- Voltage window: 2.7 to 4.3 V
- C-rate: C/1
- Temperature: 21 degC

Results (after 95 cycles):
- Initial capacity: 141 mAh/g
- Capacity retention: 72.7%
- Coulombic efficiency: 99.1%
- Impedance (Nyquist): R_ct = 72 Ohm

The energy density E = q * V relationship gives 454 Wh/kg.
Cyclic voltammetry at 0.4 mV/s showed redox peaks at
3.36/3.03 V. EIS was performed from
100 kHz to 10 mHz. Total test duration: 1812 h.
