Synthesis Report: ZnO Quantum Dot Preparation

Batch ID: SYN-124
Method: co-precipitation

Reagent preparation:
- Precursor: 172.4 mg dissolved in 16 mL deionized water
- Reducing agent: 145.3 mg in 31 mL solvent
- Stabilizer: 8.5 mg (0.025 g)

The reaction was carried out at 74 degC for 105 min
(6902 s) under continuous magnetic stirring at 347 rpm.
The pH was adjusted to 5.8 using NaOH/HCl.

After synthesis, the product was centrifuged at 6029 rpm for
21 min and washed three times with ethanol. Final product mass:
150.1 mg (yield: 51%).

Characterization:
- UV-Vis absorption peak: 604 nm
- DLS mean diameter: 9 nm
- Zeta potential: -15 mV
- XRD confirmed wurtzite crystal structure

The total reagent mass was 0.111 g. Using the Arrhenius equation
k = A * e^{-Ea/RT}, the estimated rate constant at the synthesis temperature is consistent
with literature values for Ea = 69 kJ/mol.
