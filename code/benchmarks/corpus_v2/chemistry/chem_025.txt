Reaction Kinetics Study: Saponification

Experiment ID: KIN-225

Reaction conditions:
- Temperature: 34.8 degC
- Solvent: water
- Initial concentration: 0.557 M
- Catalyst loading: 9.2 mol%

Aliquots (1.6 mL) were withdrawn at t = 0, 68,
178, 425, 1464,
and 2288 s. Conversion was monitored by GC-MS.

Kinetic analysis:
- Reaction order: pseudo-first
- Rate constant k = 3.7908e-03 s^{-1} (at 52.6 degC)
- Half-life: 3253 s (11.6 min)
- Activation energy Ea = 104 kJ/mol (from Arrhenius plot)

The Arrhenius equation k = A * e^{-Ea/RT} with pre-exponential factor
A = 9.81e+11 s^{-1} fits the data with R^2 = 0.997.
The enthalpy of activation Delta_H_ddagger was 41 kJ/mol via
Eyring analysis. Total reaction time to 95% conversion: 67 min.
Product isolated mass: 1.77 g (4302 mg).
