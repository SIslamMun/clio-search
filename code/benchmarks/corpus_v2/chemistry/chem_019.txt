Reaction Kinetics Study: Ester Hydrolysis

Experiment ID: KIN-219

Reaction conditions:
- Temperature: 54.2 degC
- Solvent: water
- Initial concentration: 0.614 M
- Catalyst loading: 2.5 mol%

Aliquots (1.0 mL) were withdrawn at t = 0, 49,
255, 576, 1339,
and 2596 s. Conversion was monitored by HPLC.

Kinetic analysis:
- Reaction order: first
- Rate constant k = 8.9763e-03 s^{-1} (at 32.7 degC)
- Half-life: 496 s (44.1 min)
- Activation energy Ea = 68 kJ/mol (from Arrhenius plot)

The Arrhenius equation k = A * e^{-Ea/RT} with pre-exponential factor
A = 4.29e+11 s^{-1} fits the data with R^2 = 0.989.
The enthalpy of activation Delta_H_ddagger was 55 kJ/mol via
Eyring analysis. Total reaction time to 95% conversion: 84 min.
Product isolated mass: 1.62 g (3204 mg).
