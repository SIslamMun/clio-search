Synthesis Report: Gold Nanoparticle Preparation

Batch ID: SYN-100
Method: sol-gel

Reagent preparation:
- Precursor: 467.9 mg dissolved in 38 mL deionized water
- Reducing agent: 192.7 mg in 31 mL solvent
- Stabilizer: 38.9 mg (0.037 g)

The reaction was carried out at 76 degC for 42 min
(3262 s) under continuous magnetic stirring at 296 rpm.
The pH was adjusted to 9.8 using NaOH/HCl.

After synthesis, the product was centrifuged at 8892 rpm for
28 min and washed three times with ethanol. Final product mass:
79.7 mg (yield: 78%).

Characterization:
- UV-Vis absorption peak: 456 nm
- DLS mean diameter: 99 nm
- Zeta potential: -21 mV
- XRD confirmed anatase crystal structure

The total reagent mass was 0.116 g. Using the Arrhenius equation
k = A * e^{-Ea/RT}, the estimated rate constant at the synthesis temperature is consistent
with literature values for Ea = 75 kJ/mol.
