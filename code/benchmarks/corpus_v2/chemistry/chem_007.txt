Reaction Kinetics Study: Ester Hydrolysis

Experiment ID: KIN-207

Reaction conditions:
- Temperature: 56.0 degC
- Solvent: DMF
- Initial concentration: 0.967 M
- Catalyst loading: 4.0 mol%

Aliquots (1.7 mL) were withdrawn at t = 0, 40,
138, 367, 1496,
and 2804 s. Conversion was monitored by HPLC.

Kinetic analysis:
- Reaction order: second
- Rate constant k = 3.8038e-03 s^{-1} (at 50.5 degC)
- Half-life: 2466 s (41.7 min)
- Activation energy Ea = 52 kJ/mol (from Arrhenius plot)

The Arrhenius equation k = A * e^{-Ea/RT} with pre-exponential factor
A = 9.98e+11 s^{-1} fits the data with R^2 = 0.974.
The enthalpy of activation Delta_H_ddagger was 59 kJ/mol via
Eyring analysis. Total reaction time to 95% conversion: 112 min.
Product isolated mass: 0.89 g (4241 mg).
