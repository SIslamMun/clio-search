Reaction Kinetics Study: Ester Hydrolysis

Experiment ID: KIN-201

Reaction conditions:
- Temperature: 43.7 degC
- Solvent: toluene
- Initial concentration: 0.955 M
- Catalyst loading: 4.6 mol%

Aliquots (1.7 mL) were withdrawn at t = 0, 33,
169, 598, 1189,
and 2440 s. Conversion was monitored by UV-Vis.

Kinetic analysis:
- Reaction order: second
- Rate constant k = 1.9388e-03 s^{-1} (at 57.2 degC)
- Half-life: 1405 s (46.6 min)
- Activation energy Ea = 64 kJ/mol (from Arrhenius plot)

The Arrhenius equation k = A * e^{-Ea/RT} with pre-exponential factor
A = 2.46e+11 s^{-1} fits the data with R^2 = 0.954.
The enthalpy of activation Delta_H_ddagger was 56 kJ/mol via
Eyring analysis. Total reaction time to 95% conversion: 58 min.
Product isolated mass: 4.03 g (1194 mg).
