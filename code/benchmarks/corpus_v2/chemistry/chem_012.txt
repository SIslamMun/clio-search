Synthesis Report: TiO2 Nanotube Preparation

Batch ID: SYN-112
Method: co-precipitation

Reagent preparation:
- Precursor: 358.1 mg dissolved in 47 mL deionized water
- Reducing agent: 169.6 mg in 27 mL solvent
- Stabilizer: 8.7 mg (0.006 g)

The reaction was carried out at 90 degC for 74 min
(4274 s) under continuous magnetic stirring at 415 rpm.
The pH was adjusted to 5.2 using NaOH/HCl.

After synthesis, the product was centrifuged at 9573 rpm for
25 min and washed three times with ethanol. Final product mass:
157.9 mg (yield: 67%).

Characterization:
- UV-Vis absorption peak: 445 nm
- DLS mean diameter: 16 nm
- Zeta potential: -19 mV
- XRD confirmed anatase crystal structure

The total reagent mass was 0.443 g. Using the Arrhenius equation
k = A * e^{-Ea/RT}, the estimated rate constant at the synthesis temperature is consistent
with literature values for Ea = 101 kJ/mol.
