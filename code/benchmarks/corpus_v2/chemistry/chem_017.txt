Gas-Phase Reaction Study: 2NO2 <-> N2O4

Reactor: static reactor with volume 2.6 L
Temperature: 597 K
Total pressure: 114 kPa (199354 Pa)

Using the ideal gas law PV = nRT:
- n = PV/RT = (186 kPa * 4.5 L) / (8.314 J/(mol*K) * 321 K)
- n = 0.4385 mol

Reactant partial pressures: 44.0 kPa each, balance N2
Residence time: 39.3 s
Conversion at exit: 31%

The rate constant was determined as k = 3.719e+01 cm^3/(molecule*s)
at 562 K, giving an Arrhenius activation energy
Ea = 21.6 kJ/mol from the relation k = A * e^{-Ea/RT}.
The pre-exponential factor A = 4.02e-10 cm^3/(molecule*s).

Product analysis by FTIR showed complete consumption of the limiting reagent.
Mass balance closure: 96.5%. Total reactant mass:
7.14 g. Reactor wall temperature uniformity: +/- 2 K.
