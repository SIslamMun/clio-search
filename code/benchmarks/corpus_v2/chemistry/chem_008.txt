Electrochemical Characterization: LiCoO2 Electrode

Cell ID: EC-308
Configuration: pouch cell

Electrode preparation:
- Active material: 3.81 mg
- Conductive additive (Super P): 0.51 mg
- Binder (PVDF): 0.29 mg
- Total electrode mass: 6.57 mg
- Active material loading: 2.9 mg/cm^2
- Electrode thickness: 61 um (0.079 mm)

Cycling conditions:
- Voltage window: 2.7 to 4.2 V
- C-rate: C/10
- Temperature: 20 degC

Results (after 225 cycles):
- Initial capacity: 159 mAh/g
- Capacity retention: 89.6%
- Coulombic efficiency: 98.3%
- Impedance (Nyquist): R_ct = 197 Ohm

The energy density E = q * V relationship gives 398 Wh/kg.
Cyclic voltammetry at 0.3 mV/s showed redox peaks at
3.26/3.22 V. EIS was performed from
100 kHz to 10 mHz. Total test duration: 1921 h.
