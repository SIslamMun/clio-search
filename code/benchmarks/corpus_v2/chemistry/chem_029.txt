Gas-Phase Reaction Study: 2NO2 <-> N2O4

Reactor: flow tube with volume 1.2 L
Temperature: 386 K
Total pressure: 111 kPa (130389 Pa)

Using the ideal gas law PV = nRT:
- n = PV/RT = (195 kPa * 1.4 L) / (8.314 J/(mol*K) * 323 K)
- n = 0.1399 mol

Reactant partial pressures: 6.9 kPa each, balance N2
Residence time: 48.5 s
Conversion at exit: 36%

The rate constant was determined as k = 7.047e+01 cm^3/(molecule*s)
at 441 K, giving an Arrhenius activation energy
Ea = 25.1 kJ/mol from the relation k = A * e^{-Ea/RT}.
The pre-exponential factor A = 8.58e-10 cm^3/(molecule*s).

Product analysis by FTIR showed complete consumption of the limiting reagent.
Mass balance closure: 95.7%. Total reactant mass:
7.26 g. Reactor wall temperature uniformity: +/- 5 K.
