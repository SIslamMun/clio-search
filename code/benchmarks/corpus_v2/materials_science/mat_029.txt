Thin Film Mechanical Properties: SiN_x on Silicon

Sample: TF-6029
Film thickness: 229 nm (0.277 mm equivalent is NOT correct; actual is sub-micron)
Substrate: Si(100), 0.6 mm thick

Nanoindentation was performed using a Berkovich tip with maximum load
5.6 mN. The Oliver-Pharr method yielded:
- Hardness: 22.9 GPa
- Reduced modulus: 246 GPa

The residual stress in the film was measured by wafer curvature (Stoney equation)
at 468 MPa compressive. The biaxial modulus was
266 GPa. Deposition was carried out via PECVD at a
chamber pressure of 93 Pa with substrate temperature
316 degC.

Process gas: SiH4/NH3/N2 mixture at total flow 224 sccm.
RF power: 64 W at 13.56 MHz. Deposition duration was
58 min, yielding a deposition rate of approximately
7.3 nm/min. Film adhesion was verified by scratch testing;
critical load was 22 mN.
