Thin Film Mechanical Properties: SiN_x on Silicon

Sample: TF-6013
Film thickness: 364 nm (0.242 mm equivalent is NOT correct; actual is sub-micron)
Substrate: Si(100), 0.5 mm thick

Nanoindentation was performed using a Berkovich tip with maximum load
8.8 mN. The Oliver-Pharr method yielded:
- Hardness: 15.5 GPa
- Reduced modulus: 215 GPa

The residual stress in the film was measured by wafer curvature (Stoney equation)
at 423 MPa compressive. The biaxial modulus was
206 GPa. Deposition was carried out via PECVD at a
chamber pressure of 159 Pa with substrate temperature
330 degC.

Process gas: SiH4/NH3/N2 mixture at total flow 122 sccm.
RF power: 79 W at 13.56 MHz. Deposition duration was
41 min, yielding a deposition rate of approximately
6.4 nm/min. Film adhesion was verified by scratch testing;
critical load was 17 mN.
