Composite Laminate Tensile Test: CFRP Layup [0/0/90/90]s

Sample ID: CMP-4011
Laminate thickness: 2.17 mm (13 plies)
Specimen width: 19.4 mm
Tab material: Glass/epoxy, bonded with cyanoacrylate adhesive

The test was conducted per ASTM D3039 at a rate of 1.5 mm/min.
Strain was measured by biaxial strain gauges with 6 mm
gauge length, logged at 1899 Hz.

Key results:
- Longitudinal tensile strength: 803 MPa
- Longitudinal modulus: 107 GPa
- Transverse tensile strength: 42 MPa
- Poisson ratio: 0.295
- Failure strain: 0.89%

Failure mode: lateral/gauge/middle (LGM) per ASTM classification. The laminate
exhibited matrix cracking as
the dominant damage mechanism. The applied stress of 635324 kPa
at failure is consistent with rule-of-mixtures predictions. Test duration was
approximately 120 s.
