Composite Laminate Tensile Test: CFRP Layup [0/0/90/90]s

Sample ID: CMP-4003
Laminate thickness: 2.03 mm (9 plies)
Specimen width: 15.5 mm
Tab material: Glass/epoxy, bonded with cyanoacrylate adhesive

The test was conducted per ASTM D3039 at a rate of 1.2 mm/min.
Strain was measured by biaxial strain gauges with 6 mm
gauge length, logged at 1319 Hz.

Key results:
- Longitudinal tensile strength: 740 MPa
- Longitudinal modulus: 68 GPa
- Transverse tensile strength: 44 MPa
- Poisson ratio: 0.324
- Failure strain: 1.24%

Failure mode: lateral/gauge/middle (LGM) per ASTM classification. The laminate
exhibited delamination as
the dominant damage mechanism. The applied stress of 813162 kPa
at failure is consistent with rule-of-mixtures predictions. Test duration was
approximately 301 s.
