Polymer Tensile Properties: Nylon 6,6 Injection-Molded Specimen

Test ID: PLY-3026
Specimen dimensions: 3.4 mm thick, 10.3 mm wide,
overall length 164 mm (ISO 527-2, Type 1A).

The specimen was conditioned at 23 degC and 50% RH for 48 hours before testing.
Testing was performed on a universal testing machine with a 9 kN
load cell at a crosshead speed of 19 mm/min.

Measured properties:
- Tensile strength: 70.2 MPa
- Yield stress: 55.2 MPa
- Strain at break: 54%
- Elastic modulus: 3.08 GPa

The stress-strain curve exhibited distinct yield point followed by cold drawing.
Neck formation was observed at approximately 10% strain.
The equivalent stress at yield, 59176 kPa, falls within
manufacturer specifications. Five replicate tests were conducted; the coefficient
of variation in UTS was 2.0%.

Duration of test: 169 s. Specimen mass: 11.8 g.
The inertial correction for the crosshead was negligible; F = m * a gives
a dynamic contribution less than 0.1% of the applied force (F = ma).
