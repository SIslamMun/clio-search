Thin Film Mechanical Properties: SiN_x on Silicon

Sample: TF-6037
Film thickness: 285 nm (0.120 mm equivalent is NOT correct; actual is sub-micron)
Substrate: Si(100), 0.5 mm thick

Nanoindentation was performed using a Berkovich tip with maximum load
2.9 mN. The Oliver-Pharr method yielded:
- Hardness: 18.3 GPa
- Reduced modulus: 226 GPa

The residual stress in the film was measured by wafer curvature (Stoney equation)
at 290 MPa compressive. The biaxial modulus was
275 GPa. Deposition was carried out via PECVD at a
chamber pressure of 175 Pa with substrate temperature
275 degC.

Process gas: SiH4/NH3/N2 mixture at total flow 116 sccm.
RF power: 53 W at 13.56 MHz. Deposition duration was
42 min, yielding a deposition rate of approximately
15.0 nm/min. Film adhesion was verified by scratch testing;
critical load was 24 mN.
