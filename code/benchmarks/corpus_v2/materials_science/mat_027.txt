Composite Laminate Tensile Test: CFRP Layup [0/0/90/90]s

Sample ID: CMP-4027
Laminate thickness: 2.93 mm (11 plies)
Specimen width: 18.6 mm
Tab material: Glass/epoxy, bonded with cyanoacrylate adhesive

The test was conducted per ASTM D3039 at a rate of 1.1 mm/min.
Strain was measured by biaxial strain gauges with 8 mm
gauge length, logged at 2321 Hz.

Key results:
- Longitudinal tensile strength: 788 MPa
- Longitudinal modulus: 128 GPa
- Transverse tensile strength: 66 MPa
- Poisson ratio: 0.301
- Failure strain: 1.17%

Failure mode: lateral/gauge/middle (LGM) per ASTM classification. The laminate
exhibited delamination as
the dominant damage mechanism. The applied stress of 795710 kPa
at failure is consistent with rule-of-mixtures predictions. Test duration was
approximately 309 s.
