Thin Film Mechanical Properties: SiN_x on Silicon

Sample: TF-6021
Film thickness: 354 nm (0.432 mm equivalent is NOT correct; actual is sub-micron)
Substrate: Si(100), 0.3 mm thick

Nanoindentation was performed using a Berkovich tip with maximum load
4.0 mN. The Oliver-Pharr method yielded:
- Hardness: 16.3 GPa
- Reduced modulus: 248 GPa

The residual stress in the film was measured by wafer curvature (Stoney equation)
at 181 MPa compressive. The biaxial modulus was
244 GPa. Deposition was carried out via PECVD at a
chamber pressure of 156 Pa with substrate temperature
306 degC.

Process gas: SiH4/NH3/N2 mixture at total flow 122 sccm.
RF power: 192 W at 13.56 MHz. Deposition duration was
48 min, yielding a deposition rate of approximately
6.5 nm/min. Film adhesion was verified by scratch testing;
critical load was 11 mN.
