Thin Film Mechanical Properties: SiN_x on Silicon

Sample: TF-6005
Film thickness: 122 nm (0.429 mm equivalent is NOT correct; actual is sub-micron)
Substrate: Si(100), 0.6 mm thick

Nanoindentation was performed using a Berkovich tip with maximum load
4.6 mN. The Oliver-Pharr method yielded:
- Hardness: 15.7 GPa
- Reduced modulus: 241 GPa

The residual stress in the film was measured by wafer curvature (Stoney equation)
at 384 MPa compressive. The biaxial modulus was
272 GPa. Deposition was carried out via PECVD at a
chamber pressure of 82 Pa with substrate temperature
300 degC.

Process gas: SiH4/NH3/N2 mixture at total flow 277 sccm.
RF power: 146 W at 13.56 MHz. Deposition duration was
26 min, yielding a deposition rate of approximately
6.4 nm/min. Film adhesion was verified by scratch testing;
critical load was 40 mN.
