Composite Laminate Tensile Test: CFRP Layup [0/45/-45/90]s

Sample ID: CMP-4035
Laminate thickness: 2.37 mm (13 plies)
Specimen width: 18.0 mm
Tab material: Glass/epoxy, bonded with cyanoacrylate adhesive

The test was conducted per ASTM D3039 at a rate of 1.4 mm/min.
Strain was measured by biaxial strain gauges with 9 mm
gauge length, logged at 4343 Hz.

Key results:
- Longitudinal tensile strength: 923 MPa
- Longitudinal modulus: 119 GPa
- Transverse tensile strength: 70 MPa
- Poisson ratio: 0.343
- Failure strain: 1.39%

Failure mode: lateral/gauge/middle (LGM) per ASTM classification. The laminate
exhibited delamination as
the dominant damage mechanism. The applied stress of 861502 kPa
at failure is consistent with rule-of-mixtures predictions. Test duration was
approximately 109 s.
