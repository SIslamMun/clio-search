Composite Laminate Tensile Test: CFRP Layup [0/90/0]s

Sample ID: CMP-4019
Laminate thickness: 2.86 mm (13 plies)
Specimen width: 17.8 mm
Tab material: Glass/epoxy, bonded with cyanoacrylate adhesive

The test was conducted per ASTM D3039 at a rate of 1.4 mm/min.
Strain was measured by biaxial strain gauges with 8 mm
gauge length, logged at 2210 Hz.

Key results:
- Longitudinal tensile strength: 1190 MPa
- Longitudinal modulus: 125 GPa
- Transverse tensile strength: 56 MPa
- Poisson ratio: 0.327
- Failure strain: 1.47%

Failure mode: lateral/gauge/middle (LGM) per ASTM classification. The laminate
exhibited matrix cracking as
the dominant damage mechanism. The applied stress of 662152 kPa
at failure is consistent with rule-of-mixtures predictions. Test duration was
approximately 363 s.
