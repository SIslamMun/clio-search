Atmospheric Boundary Layer Study: Campaign ABL-534

Site: Flat terrain, fetch > 5 km, crop stubble surface
Measurement period: 10 days in autumn

Tower instrumentation (10 levels from 2 m to 60 m):
- Sonic anemometers: 3-D wind at 20 Hz
- Temperature/humidity: aspirated thermohygrometers
- Pressure: Vaisala PTB330 at 2 m, accuracy +/- 0.1 hPa

Selected results (convective afternoon case):
- Mixed layer depth: 1122 m
- Surface heat flux: 299 W/m^2
- Friction velocity u*: 0.57 m/s
- 10-m wind speed: 4.6 m/s
- Surface pressure: 99.9 kPa
- Roughness length z0: 0.070 m
- Monin-Obukhov length: -108 m (unstable)

Turbulence statistics: the velocity variance sigma_u was 1.56 m/s
and the turbulent kinetic energy TKE = 0.5*(sigma_u^2 + sigma_v^2 + sigma_w^2)
was approximately 0.71 m^2/s^2. The surface pressure
in Pa was 99354 Pa with a diurnal range of
357 Pa.

The thermal wind estimated from the pressure gradient was 2.3 m/s
at 1 km height. Entrainment zone thickness: 151 m.
