Atmospheric Boundary Layer Study: Campaign ABL-524

Site: Flat terrain, fetch > 5 km, crop stubble surface
Measurement period: 12 days in summer

Tower instrumentation (10 levels from 2 m to 60 m):
- Sonic anemometers: 3-D wind at 10 Hz
- Temperature/humidity: aspirated thermohygrometers
- Pressure: Vaisala PTB330 at 2 m, accuracy +/- 0.1 hPa

Selected results (convective afternoon case):
- Mixed layer depth: 1645 m
- Surface heat flux: 366 W/m^2
- Friction velocity u*: 0.37 m/s
- 10-m wind speed: 7.1 m/s
- Surface pressure: 98.4 kPa
- Roughness length z0: 0.076 m
- Monin-Obukhov length: -191 m (unstable)

Turbulence statistics: the velocity variance sigma_u was 0.97 m/s
and the turbulent kinetic energy TKE = 0.5*(sigma_u^2 + sigma_v^2 + sigma_w^2)
was approximately 1.14 m^2/s^2. The surface pressure
in Pa was 101722 Pa with a diurnal range of
375 Pa.

The thermal wind estimated from the pressure gradient was 2.9 m/s
at 1 km height. Entrainment zone thickness: 136 m.
