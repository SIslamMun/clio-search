Atmospheric Boundary Layer Study: Campaign ABL-504

Site: Flat terrain, fetch > 5 km, crop stubble surface
Measurement period: 13 days in spring

Tower instrumentation (10 levels from 2 m to 60 m):
- Sonic anemometers: 3-D wind at 10 Hz
- Temperature/humidity: aspirated thermohygrometers
- Pressure: Vaisala PTB330 at 2 m, accuracy +/- 0.1 hPa

Selected results (convective afternoon case):
- Mixed layer depth: 2309 m
- Surface heat flux: 272 W/m^2
- Friction velocity u*: 0.39 m/s
- 10-m wind speed: 5.2 m/s
- Surface pressure: 97.1 kPa
- Roughness length z0: 0.015 m
- Monin-Obukhov length: -473 m (unstable)

Turbulence statistics: the velocity variance sigma_u was 1.22 m/s
and the turbulent kinetic energy TKE = 0.5*(sigma_u^2 + sigma_v^2 + sigma_w^2)
was approximately 2.56 m^2/s^2. The surface pressure
in Pa was 98404 Pa with a diurnal range of
130 Pa.

The thermal wind estimated from the pressure gradient was 3.5 m/s
at 1 km height. Entrainment zone thickness: 121 m.
