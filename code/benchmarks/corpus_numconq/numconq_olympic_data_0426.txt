Israil Magomedgireyevich Arsamakov, a Male athlete from Soviet Union (URS), competed in the 1988 Summer Olympics in Seoul. At 26 years old, Israil Magomedgireyevich Arsamakov participated in Weightlifting - Weightlifting Men's Light-Heavyweight, standing at 169 cm tall and weighing 85 kg, and won the Gold medal.
