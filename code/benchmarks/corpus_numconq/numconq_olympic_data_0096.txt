Christine M. "Crissy" Ahmann-Leighton (-Perham), a Female athlete from United States (USA), competed in the 1992 Summer Olympics in Barcelona. At 22 years old, Christine M. "Crissy" Ahmann-Leighton (-Perham) participated in Swimming - Swimming Women's 4 x 100 metres Freestyle Relay, standing at 172 cm tall and weighing 52 kg, and won the Gold medal.
