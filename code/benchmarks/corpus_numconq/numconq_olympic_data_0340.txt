Tatyana Mikhaylovna Anisimova (Poluboyarova-), a 26-year-old Female athlete from Soviet Union (URS), competed in the 1976 Summer Olympics held in Montreal. Participating in Athletics - Athletics Women's 100 metres Hurdles, Tatyana Mikhaylovna Anisimova (Poluboyarova-) stood at 172 cm tall, weighed 65 kg, and won the Silver medal.
