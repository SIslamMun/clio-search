Since its inception in 2006, Serie A has been a cornerstone of Cricket in England. With 29 teams, including the celebrated AC Milan, the league has become a global sensation. It generates an astounding 4,796,460,000.0 USD annually, and players enjoy an average salary of 14,090,000.0 USD. The league's viewership, reaching 47.52 million, is a testament to its enduring popularity.
