In Montreal during the 1976 Summer Olympics, Olga Fyodorovna Barysheva-Korostelyova, a Female athlete aged 21 from Soviet Union (URS), took part in Basketball - Basketball Women's Basketball. With a height of 168 cm and a weight of 67 kg, Olga Fyodorovna Barysheva-Korostelyova clinched the Gold medal.
