Yelizaveta Petrovna Bagryantseva (-Verkhoshanskaya), a 31-year-old Female athlete from Soviet Union (URS), competed in the 1952 Summer Olympics held in Helsinki. Participating in Athletics - Athletics Women's Discus Throw, Yelizaveta Petrovna Bagryantseva (-Verkhoshanskaya) stood at 170 cm tall, weighed 76 kg, and won the Silver medal.
