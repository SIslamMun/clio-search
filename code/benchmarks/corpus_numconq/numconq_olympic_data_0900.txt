Yevgeny Prokopyevich Belyayev, a 21-year-old Male athlete from Soviet Union (URS), competed in the 1976 Winter Olympics held in Innsbruck. Participating in Cross Country Skiing - Cross Country Skiing Men's 15 kilometres, Yevgeny Prokopyevich Belyayev stood at 188 cm tall, weighed 91 kg, and won the Silver medal.
