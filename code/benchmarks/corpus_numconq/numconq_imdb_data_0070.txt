Jodaeiye Nader az Simin, released in 2011, is a Drama film directed by Asghar Farhadi. With a runtime of 123 min, it earned a PG-13 certificate and has an IMDB rating of 8.3. The movie, starring Payman Maadi, Leila Hatami, Sareh Bayat, and Shahab Hosseini, received a Meta score of 95. Its story revolves around A married couple are faced with a difficult decision - to improve the life of their child by moving to another country or to stay in Iran and look after a deteriorating parent who has Alzheimer's disease.. It garnered 220002 votes and grossed 7098492 at the box office.
