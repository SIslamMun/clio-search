Yelena Rabigovna Akhaminova (-Sokolovskaya), a Female athlete aged 18 from Soviet Union (URS), participated in the 1980 Summer Olympics in Moskva. Competing in Volleyball - Volleyball Women's Volleyball, Yelena Rabigovna Akhaminova (-Sokolovskaya) was 181 cm tall, weighed 80 kg, and earned the Gold medal.
