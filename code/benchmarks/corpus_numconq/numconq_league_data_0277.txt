Since its inception in 1921, Bundesliga has been a cornerstone of Basketball in Spain. With 21 teams, including the celebrated Borussia Dortmund, the league has become a global sensation. It generates an astounding 6,234,380,000.0 USD annually, and players enjoy an average salary of 13,380,000.0 USD. The league's viewership, reaching 226.6 million, is a testament to its enduring popularity.
