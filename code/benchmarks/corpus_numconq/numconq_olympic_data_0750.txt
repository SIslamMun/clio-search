Yelena Nikolayevna Batukhtina-Tyurina, a 21-year-old Female athlete from Unified Team (EUN), competed in the 1992 Summer Olympics held in Barcelona. Participating in Volleyball - Volleyball Women's Volleyball, Yelena Nikolayevna Batukhtina-Tyurina stood at 184 cm tall, weighed 82 kg, and won the Silver medal.
