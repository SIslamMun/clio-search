Since its inception in 1887, La Liga has been a cornerstone of Cricket in Spain. With 30 teams, including the celebrated Barcelona, the league has become a global sensation. It generates an astounding 6,271,190,000.0 USD annually, and players enjoy an average salary of 13,000,000.0 USD. The league's viewership, reaching 488.14 million, is a testament to its enduring popularity.
