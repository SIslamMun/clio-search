La vie d'Adèle, a Drama, Romance film from 2013, was directed by Abdellatif Kechiche. With a runtime of 180 min and a A certificate, it has an IMDB rating of 7.7 and a Meta score of 89. The movie stars Léa Seydoux, Adèle Exarchopoulos, Salim Kechiouche, and Aurélien Recoing, and its plot is: Adèle's life is changed when she meets Emma, a young woman with blue hair, who will allow her to discover desire and to assert herself as a woman and as an adult. In front of others, Adèle grows, seeks herself, loses herself, and ultimately finds herself through love and loss.. It garnered 138741 votes and grossed 2199675.
