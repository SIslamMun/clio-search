During the 1976 Winter Olympics in Innsbruck, Tatyana Borisovna Averina-Barabash, a 25-year-old Female athlete representing Soviet Union (URS), competed in Speed Skating - Speed Skating Women's 500 metres. With a height of 165 cm and a weight of 61 kg, Tatyana Borisovna Averina-Barabash secured the Bronze medal.
