Vitalina Igorevna Batsarashkina, a Female athlete from Russia (RUS), competed in the 2016 Summer Olympics in Rio de Janeiro. At 19 years old, Vitalina Igorevna Batsarashkina participated in Shooting - Shooting Women's Air Pistol, 10 metres, standing at 162 cm tall and weighing 60 kg, and won the Silver medal.
