Yuliya Vladimirovna Barsukova (-Samokhin), a Female athlete from Russia (RUS), competed in the 2000 Summer Olympics in Sydney. At 21 years old, Yuliya Vladimirovna Barsukova (-Samokhin) participated in Rhythmic Gymnastics - Rhythmic Gymnastics Women's Individual, standing at 168 cm tall and weighing 45 kg, and won the Gold medal.
