Hable con ella, released in 2002, is a Drama, Mystery, Romance film directed by Pedro Almodóvar. With a runtime of 112 min, it earned a R certificate and has an IMDB rating of 7.9. The movie, starring Rosario Flores, Javier Cámara, Darío Grandinetti, and Leonor Watling, received a Meta score of 86. Its story revolves around Two men share an odd friendship while they care for two women who are both in deep comas.. It garnered 104691 votes and grossed 9284265 at the box office.
