Dunkirk, released in 2017, is a Action, Drama, History film directed by Christopher Nolan. With a runtime of 106 min, it earned a UA certificate and has an IMDB rating of 7.8. The movie, starring Fionn Whitehead, Barry Keoghan, Mark Rylance, and Tom Hardy, received a Meta score of 94. Its story revolves around Allied soldiers from Belgium, the British Empire, and France are surrounded by the German Army and evacuated during a fierce battle in World War II..
