At the 1980 Summer Olympics in Moskva, Magomedgasan Mingazhutdinovich Abushev, a 20-year-old Male athlete from Soviet Union (URS), competed in Wrestling - Wrestling Men's Featherweight, Freestyle. Standing at 170 cm and weighing 62 kg, Magomedgasan Mingazhutdinovich Abushev was awarded the Gold medal.
