Yevgeniya Aleksandrovna Bochkaryova, a 16-year-old Female athlete from Russia (RUS), competed in the 1996 Summer Olympics held in Atlanta. Participating in Rhythmic Gymnastics - Rhythmic Gymnastics Women's Group, Yevgeniya Aleksandrovna Bochkaryova stood at 160 cm tall, weighed 40 kg, and won the Bronze medal.
