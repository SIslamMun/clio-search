Blood Simple, released in 1984, is a Crime, Drama, Thriller film directed by Joel Coen. With a runtime of 99 min, it earned a A certificate and has an IMDB rating of 7.6. The movie, starring Ethan Coen, John Getz, Frances McDormand, and Dan Hedaya, received a Meta score of 82. Its story revolves around The owner of a seedy small-town Texas bar discovers that one of his employees is having an affair with his wife. A chaotic chain of misunderstandings, lies and mischief ensues after he devises a plot to have them murdered.. It garnered 87745 votes and grossed 2150000 at the box office.
