Crna macka, beli macor, released in 1998, is a Comedy, Crime, Romance film directed by Emir Kusturica. With a runtime of 127 min, it earned a R certificate and has an IMDB rating of 8.1. The movie, starring Bajram Severdzan, Srdjan 'Zika' Todorovic, Branka Katic, and Florijan Ajdini, received a Meta score of 73. Its story revolves around Matko and his son Zare live on the banks of the Danube river and get by through hustling and basically doing anything to make a living. In order to pay off a business debt Matko agrees to marry off Zare to the sister of a local gangster.. It garnered 50862 votes and grossed 348660 at the box office.
