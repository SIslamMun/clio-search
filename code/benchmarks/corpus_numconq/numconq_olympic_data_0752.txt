Yanina Farkhadovna "Yana" Batyrshina, a Female athlete aged 16 from Russia (RUS), participated in the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta. Competing in Rhythmic Gymnastics - Rhythmic Gymnastics Women's Individual, Yanina Farkhadovna "Yana" Batyrshina was 163 cm tall, weighed 47 kg, and earned the Silver medal.
