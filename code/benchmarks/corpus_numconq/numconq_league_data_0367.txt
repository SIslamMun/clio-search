Since its inception in 1888, Serie A has been a cornerstone of Cricket in Germany. With 29 teams, including the celebrated Inter Milan, the league has become a global sensation. It generates an astounding 4,158,660,000.0 USD annually, and players enjoy an average salary of 2,900,000.0 USD. The league's viewership, reaching 459.1 million, is a testament to its enduring popularity.
