The Wizard of Oz, released in 1939, is a Adventure, Family, Fantasy film directed by Victor Fleming. With a runtime of 102 min, it earned a U certificate and has an IMDB rating of 8.0. The movie, starring George Cukor, Mervyn LeRoy, Norman Taurog, and Richard Thorpe, received a Meta score of 92. Its story revolves around Dorothy Gale is swept away from a farm in Kansas to a magical land of Oz in a tornado and embarks on a quest with her new friends to see the Wizard who can help her return home to Kansas and help her friends as well.. It garnered 371379 votes and grossed 2076020 at the box office.
