Linda Widwey Andersen (-Cerup-Simonsen), a 23-year-old Female athlete from Norway (NOR), competed in the 1992 Summer Olympics held in Barcelona. Participating in Sailing - Sailing Women's One Person Dinghy, Linda Widwey Andersen (-Cerup-Simonsen) stood at 169 cm tall, weighed 64 kg, and won the Gold medal.
