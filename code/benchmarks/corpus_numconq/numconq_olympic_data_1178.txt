Olga Petrovna Bondarenko (Krentser-), a Female athlete aged 28 from Soviet Union (URS), participated in the 1988 Summer Olympics held in Seoul. Competing in Athletics - Athletics Women's 10,000 metres, Olga Petrovna Bondarenko (Krentser-) was 154 cm tall, weighed 42 kg, and took home the Gold medal.
