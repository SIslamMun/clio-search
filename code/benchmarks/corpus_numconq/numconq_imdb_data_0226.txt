The Drama, Romance film Slumdog Millionaire, directed by Danny Boyle, was released in 2008. It has a runtime of 120 min and a UA certificate. With an IMDB rating of 8.0 and a Meta score of 84, the movie features Loveleen Tandan, Dev Patel, Freida Pinto, and Saurabh Shukla. Its plot is summarized as: A Mumbai teenager reflects on his life after being accused of cheating on the Indian version of "Who Wants to be a Millionaire?".. It garnered 798882 votes and grossed 141319928.
