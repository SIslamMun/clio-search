Doctor Zhivago, released in 1965, is a Drama, Romance, War film directed by David Lean. With a runtime of 197 min, it earned a A certificate and has an IMDB rating of 8.0. The movie, starring Omar Sharif, Julie Christie, Geraldine Chaplin, and Rod Steiger, received a Meta score of 69. Its story revolves around The life of a Russian physician and poet who, although married to another, falls in love with a political activist's wife and experiences hardship during World War I and then the October Revolution..
