At the 1992 Winter Olympics in Albertville, Yuliya Yakunovna Allagulova, a 19-year-old Female athlete from Unified Team (EUN), competed in Short Track Speed Skating - Short Track Speed Skating Women's 3,000 metres Relay. Standing at 176 cm and weighing 54 kg, Yuliya Yakunovna Allagulova achieved the Bronze medal.
