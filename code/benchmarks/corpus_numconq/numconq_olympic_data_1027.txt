During the 1960 Winter Olympics in Squaw Valley, Heidi Biebl (-Schmid, -Prelevi), a 19-year-old Female athlete representing Germany (GER), competed in Alpine Skiing - Alpine Skiing Women's Downhill. With a height of 159 cm and a weight of 56 kg, Heidi Biebl (-Schmid, -Prelevi) secured the Gold medal.
