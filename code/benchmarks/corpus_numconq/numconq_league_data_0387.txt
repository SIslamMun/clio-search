Since its inception in 1905, La Liga has been a cornerstone of Cricket in Spain. With 24 teams, including the celebrated Real Madrid, the league has become a global sensation. It generates an astounding 1,683,380,000.0 USD annually, and players enjoy an average salary of 13,060,000.0 USD. The league's viewership, reaching 179.68 million, is a testament to its enduring popularity.
