Maria Yolande Caroline Gertrude "Marjolein" Bolhuis-Eijsvogel, a Female athlete from Netherlands (NED), competed in the 1984 Summer Olympics in Los Angeles. At 23 years old, Maria Yolande Caroline Gertrude "Marjolein" Bolhuis-Eijsvogel participated in Hockey - Hockey Women's Hockey, standing at 168 cm tall and weighing 63 kg, and won the Gold medal.
