Since its inception in 1982, La Liga has been a cornerstone of Football in France. With 18 teams, including the celebrated Barcelona, the league has become a global sensation. It generates an astounding 2,812,760,000.0 USD annually, and players enjoy an average salary of 7,780,000.0 USD. The league's viewership, reaching 331.23 million, is a testament to its enduring popularity.
