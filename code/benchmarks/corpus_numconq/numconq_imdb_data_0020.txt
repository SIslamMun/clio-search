Cidade de Deus, released in 2002, is a Crime, Drama film directed by Fernando Meirelles. With a runtime of 130 min, it earned a A certificate and has an IMDB rating of 8.6. The movie, starring Kátia Lund, Alexandre Rodrigues, Leandro Firmino, and Matheus Nachtergaele, received a Meta score of 79. Its story revolves around In the slums of Rio, two kids' paths diverge as one struggles to become a photographer and the other a kingpin.. It garnered 699256 votes and grossed 7563397 at the box office.
