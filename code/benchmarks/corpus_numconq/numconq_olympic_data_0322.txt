Yelena Valentinovna Andreyuk (-Chesnokova), a Female athlete aged 21 from Soviet Union (URS), participated in the 1980 Summer Olympics in Moskva. Competing in Volleyball - Volleyball Women's Volleyball, Yelena Valentinovna Andreyuk (-Chesnokova) was 180 cm tall, weighed 85 kg, and earned the Gold medal.
