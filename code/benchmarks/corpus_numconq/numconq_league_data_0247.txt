Since its inception in 1996, Bundesliga has been a cornerstone of Cricket in Spain. With 10 teams, including the celebrated RB Leipzig, the league has become a global sensation. It generates an astounding 6,214,410,000.0 USD annually, and players enjoy an average salary of 13,360,000.0 USD. The league's viewership, reaching 171.07 million, is a testament to its enduring popularity.
