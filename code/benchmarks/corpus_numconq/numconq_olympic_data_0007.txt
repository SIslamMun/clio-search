During the 2008 Summer Olympics in Beijing, Mariya Vasilyevna Abakumova (-Tarabina), a 22-year-old Female athlete representing Russia (RUS), competed in Athletics - Athletics Women's Javelin Throw. With a height of 179 cm and a weight of 80 kg, Mariya Vasilyevna Abakumova (-Tarabina) secured the Silver medal.
