En man som heter Ove, released in 2015, is a Comedy, Drama, Romance film directed by Hannes Holm. With a runtime of 116 min, it earned a PG-13 certificate and has an IMDB rating of 7.7. The movie, starring Rolf Lassgård, Bahar Pars, Filip Berg, and Ida Engvoll, received a Meta score of 70. Its story revolves around Ove, an ill-tempered, isolated retiree who spends his days enforcing block association rules and visiting his wife's grave, has finally given up on life just as an unlikely friendship develops with his boisterous new neighbors.. It garnered 47444 votes and grossed 3358518 at the box office.
