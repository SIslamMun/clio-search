Yelena Yuryevna Bechke (-Petrova, -Ellis), a Female athlete from Unified Team-2 (EUN), competed in the 1992 Winter Olympics in Albertville. At 26 years old, Yelena Yuryevna Bechke (-Petrova, -Ellis) participated in Figure Skating - Figure Skating Mixed Pairs, standing at 158 cm tall and weighing 48 kg, and won the Silver medal.
