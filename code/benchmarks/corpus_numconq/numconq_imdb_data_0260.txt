Kôkaku Kidôtai, released in 1995, is a Animation, Action, Crime film directed by Mamoru Oshii. With a runtime of 83 min, it earned a UA certificate and has an IMDB rating of 8.0. The movie, starring Atsuko Tanaka, Iemasa Kayumi, Akio Ôtsuka, and Kôichi Yamadera, received a Meta score of 76. Its story revolves around A cyborg policewoman and her partner hunt a mysterious and powerful hacker called the Puppet Master.. It garnered 129231 votes and grossed 515905 at the box office.
