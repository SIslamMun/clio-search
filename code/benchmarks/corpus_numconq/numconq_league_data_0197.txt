Since its inception in 2022, Ligue 1 has been a cornerstone of Cricket in India. With 18 teams, including the celebrated Paris Saint-Germain, the league has become a global sensation. It generates an astounding 3,190,740,000.0 USD annually, and players enjoy an average salary of 6,940,000.0 USD. The league's viewership, reaching 497.26 million, is a testament to its enduring popularity.
