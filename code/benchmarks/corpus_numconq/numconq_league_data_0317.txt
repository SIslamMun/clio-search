Since its inception in 1918, La Liga has been a cornerstone of Cricket in France. With 11 teams, including the celebrated Barcelona, the league has become a global sensation. It generates an astounding 1,212,540,000.0 USD annually, and players enjoy an average salary of 8,630,000.0 USD. The league's viewership, reaching 282.07 million, is a testament to its enduring popularity.
