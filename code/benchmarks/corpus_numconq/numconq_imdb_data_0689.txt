My Cousin Vinny, released in 1992, is a Comedy, Crime film directed by Jonathan Lynn. With a runtime of 120 min, it earned a UA certificate and has an IMDB rating of 7.6. The movie, starring Joe Pesci, Marisa Tomei, Ralph Macchio, and Mitchell Whitfield, received a Meta score of 68. Its story revolves around Two New Yorkers accused of murder in rural Alabama while on their way back to college call in the help of one of their cousins, a loudmouth lawyer with no trial experience.. It garnered 107325 votes and grossed 52929168 at the box office.
