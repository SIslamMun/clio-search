Released in 2003, Kal Ho Naa Ho is a Comedy, Drama, Musical film directed by Nikkhil Advani. It has a runtime of 186 min and a U certificate. The movie boasts an IMDB rating of 8.0 and a Meta score of 54. Featuring stars like Preity Zinta, Shah Rukh Khan, Saif Ali Khan, and Jaya Bachchan, its plot is: Naina, an introverted, perpetually depressed girl's life changes when she meets Aman. But Aman has a secret of his own which changes their lives forever. Embroiled in all this is Rohit, Naina's best friend who conceals his love for her.. It garnered 63460 votes and grossed 1787378.
