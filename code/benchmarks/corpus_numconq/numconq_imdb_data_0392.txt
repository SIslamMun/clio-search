The Lunchbox, a Drama, Romance film from 2013, was directed by Ritesh Batra. It runs for 104 min and carries a U certificate. The movie has an IMDB rating of 7.8 and a Meta score of 76. Featuring Irrfan Khan, Nimrat Kaur, Nawazuddin Siddiqui, and Lillete Dubey, its story is summarized as: A mistaken delivery in Mumbai's famously efficient lunchbox delivery system connects a young housewife to an older man in the dusk of his life as they build a fantasy world together through notes in the lunchbox..
